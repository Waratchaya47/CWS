Chiang Mai Cultural Night

About the Event

Chiang Mai Cultural Night is an event that showcases the rich culture of Chiang Mai. The event will feature traditional music, dance, and food. There will also be a market selling local handicrafts and souvenirs. Don't miss this opportunity to experience the beauty of Chiang Mai's culture!
